Privacy Policy

Last updated: 15 January 2026

QR Code Scanner ("we", "our", or "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use our mobile application ("App").

Information Collection and Use

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information on our servers.

All QR code and barcode scan results are processed locally on your device.

Data Stored on Your Device

The App may store the following data locally on your device:

  • Scanned QR codes and barcode results
  • Scan history
  • Favorite items

This data remains on your device unless you delete it manually.

Permissions Used

Camera Permission

The App requires camera access to scan QR codes and barcodes.
The camera is used only for scanning purposes and no images or videos are recorded or transmitted.

Storage Permission

Storage access is used to:

  • Save generated QR codes
  • Download QR or barcode images
  • Share QR or barcode images

The App does not access personal files or unrelated data.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device information
  • Advertising ID
  • Approximate location (non-precise)

This data is collected and processed by Google according to their Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

We do not have access to or control over data collected by AdMob.
